Welcome to Infinity

Big Room Studios created the Infinity Development Consulting process to meet a need expressed by many entrepreneurs, companies and organizations of documenting ideas and creating an actionable plan for new technology projects. During your Infinity experience, we will help you organize and examine your product ideas and requirements, developing a solid project plan that is ready for implementation.

Ignition

We will kick-off the process with a 3 hour planning and strategy session, where we get all your ideas, features and user stories out on the table, while discussing your overall business and development goals. We then write the Ignition Document, organizing and outlining this content for your review.

Flight Plan

During this phase, the plan created in the Ignition Document is transformed into an Information Architecture document and Wireframes of key screens, allowing you to visualize user flows for your new technology project.

Launch

In this last phase, we combine your Information Architecture, Designs, User Flows and Feature Descriptions with a Technical Development Plan, involving a detailed Sprint Schedule with actionable Development Tasks. This final Launch Document will also include a proposed project budget and timeline.
